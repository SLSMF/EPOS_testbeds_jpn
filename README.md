# Geo-Inquire Jupyter Notebook at Ligure Testbed

The aim of this notebook is to provide examples of interoperability of different datasets held by different European Research Infrastructures Constoriums such as [EPOS](https://www.epos-eu.org/) and [EMSO](https://emso.eu/). This particular notebook is focused on the Ligure Testbed Testbed in the [Geo-Inquire project](https://www.geo-inquire.eu/) which is facilitating improved access to a wide range of scientific research products and services. 

Geo-INQUIRE is funded by the European Commission under project number 101058518 within the HORIZON-INFRA-2021-SERV-01 call.
